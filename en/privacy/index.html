Privacy Policy

Last updated on2018-08-26 2 min read

This privacy policy covers how this site treats personal information it receives from its visitors.

Personal data protection

As a static site it doesn’t itself collect data, uses cookies nor requires a registration but the site

  • uses Google Analytics;

  • is hosted on GitHub Pages, Google Fonts and CDNJS.

Its worth looking at

  • GitHub Privacy Policy covers GitHub Pages;

  • Google Privacy Policy covers both Google Analytics and Google Fonts;

  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy covers CDNJS.

However, no piece of information will be shared with anyone except the occasional publishing of anonymous aggregated statistics on the site itself.

Cookies

A cookie is a small text file that a website places on a visitor’s computer or device when they access the site. These files allow the site to remember actions and preferences for a period of time in to avoid the visitor having to indicate it all over again on any subsequent visits.

Cookies can be controlled and/or added or deleted as you wish. They can be deleted and most browsers can be set up to prevent them from being placed. However, it will be necessary to re-set up the preferences each time the site is accessed.

This static site itself does not use cookies, but any of the services that host its contents can. It is worth reviewing the privacy policies of each of them.
