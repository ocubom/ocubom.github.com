Biography

The responsible is Oscar Cubo, a Computer Science Engineer (Facultad de Informática, UPM) who lives in Madrid.

The journey through computers began at the age of 7 with the Inves Spectrum +, a gift that cames with an encyclopedia their parents bought. In a very short time, the mythical games fell short and he started programming his own game (a conversational adventure in text mode) in BASIC. Then came the PCs, five years (1998 - 2003) of Computer Science Engineering and the courses for the PhD in Computer Science until the Advanced Studies Certificate (2005).

Currently, he is the Technical Director of the CeSViMa, providing various services for both research and the university community. Previously, he was the Head of the center’s systems team whose main task was the administration of the Magerit supercomputer.
